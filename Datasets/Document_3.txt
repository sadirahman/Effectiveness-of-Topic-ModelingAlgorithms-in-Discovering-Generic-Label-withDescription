Drug addiction is a dependence syndrome. It is a condition where a person feels a strong desire to consume drugs and can’t do without them.
The feeling to consume it is more important for them than other daily chores and even their family
If the addicted one does not use it for longer time he is likely to feel depressed and isolated.
Addiction is the state where mind and body just cannot do without it.
The brain changes are persistent that is why drug addiction is often defined as a form of mental disorder.
